Dear Parents and Students,

We are thrilled to welcome everyone back to Forest International School Paris for the 2024-2025 academic year!

## What's New This Year

This year, we are introducing several exciting new initiatives:

- **Extended Forest Activities** - More time in nature with enhanced outdoor learning programs
- **New Art Program** - Weekly art workshops with visiting artists
- **STEM Enrichment** - Hands-on science and technology projects

- **First Day of School**: September 2, 2024
- **Parent Information Evening**: September 10, 2024
- **Fall Break**: October 21-November 1, 2024
